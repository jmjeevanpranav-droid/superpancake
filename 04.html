Operational Mechanics

How the Scheme Worked

7 Key Stages of the Fraud
1

Legitimacy

Acquired a genuine stamp vendor license in 1994 to create a legal front for operations.

2

Infiltration

Cultivated and bribed key officials at Nashik Security Press to gain insider access.

3

Tech Acquisition

Procured obsolete government printing presses and original dies for authenticity.

4

Mass Production

Printed high-quality forgeries that mimicked security features almost perfectly.

5

Artificial Shortage

Bribed officials to under-supply genuine papers, creating a desperate market demand.

6

Distribution

Built a network of ~350 agents across 12+ states to flood the market.

7

Bulk Sales

Sold directly to banks, insurance firms, and brokers without verification.

Critical Insight

The scam succeeded by exploiting decentralized, outdated systems. The artificial shortage forced buyers to skip due diligence and buy from any available source.
