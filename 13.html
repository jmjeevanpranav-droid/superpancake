Executive Summary

Key Takeaways for Future Managers

"The Telgi scam highlights that unchecked fraud at any level can devastate entire systems. Integrity and oversight are non-negotiable."

"The Telgi scam highlights that unchecked fraud at any level can devastate entire systems. Integrity and oversight are non-negotiable."

01

Ethics are Non-Negotiable

Integrity must trump short-term profit. When business decisions are driven solely by greed, the door to massive systemic fraud is opened.

02

Build Layered Controls

Single points of failure are fatal. Implement robust procurement checks, regular audits, and physical inventory tracking to prevent leakage.

03

Leverage Technology

Outdated systems invite forgery. Use digital records, unique identifiers, and continuous tech upgrades (like e-Stamping) to secure assets.

04

Coordinate Oversight

Silos protect criminals. Break down barriers between departments with data-driven collaboration to spot cross-functional anomalies.

05

Rigorous Due Diligence

Never accept vendors or documents at face value. Verify sources, especially when "deals" (like bulk discounts) seem too good to be true.

06

Watch for Red Flags

Train teams to spot early warning signals: unexplained shortages, unusual pricing, or sudden spikes in transaction volume.
