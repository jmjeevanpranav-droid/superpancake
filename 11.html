Lessons Learned: People & Ethics

Strengthening the human firewall through protection, training, and deterrence

Whistleblower Support

The Telgi scam was exposed by an insider tip. Encouraging employees to report irregularities through safe, anonymous channels is crucial for early fraud detection.

Protection & Anonymity Critical for Early Detection

Ethics & Training

Regular training raises awareness of fraud risks. When officials and employees understand the consequences of corruption, they are empowered to refuse illicit demands.

Culture of Integrity Empowering Good Decisions

Legal Deterrence

Harsh penalties—like Telgi's multi-decade imprisonment—and strict enforcement of laws like the Stamp Act reinforce the message that fraud will not be tolerated.

Zero Tolerance Strict Legal Enforcement

Strong laws and safe reporting channels can stop schemes before they grow into systemic failures.
